Make Code Easy is an Ed-Tech platform designed to simplify coding, aptitude, resume building, mock interviews, and placement strategies for engineering students—especially those from non-computer science backgrounds. We understand how overwhelming coding can feel when you’re just starting out, so we’ve built a structured, student-friendly learning ecosystem that takes you from absolute basics to advanced coding skills with confidence.

Our Mission

To transform the way engineering students learn coding and aptitude by making it simple, structured, and accessible to all—especially those from non-computer science backgrounds.

  • 🎯 Break the fear of coding by starting from basics.

  • 🤝 Build a supportive learning community with mentors & peers.

Background of Our Founder & CEO

Coming from a biological science background, I faced significant challenges when I first entered the world of coding in my 1st year of engineering. With fear and uncertainty, I stepped into my very first internship in Cyber Security & Ethical Hacking from IIT Roorkee, which became a turning point in my journey.

  • 💻 Google Data Analytics Intern
  • 🎓 Campus Ambassador at IIT Bombay
  • 🏅 Bronze Medalist at IIT Bombay
  • 🎓 Campus Ambassador at IIT Delhi
  • 🌍 Active Member of Google Developer Student Clubs
  • ⭐ Face of Internshala at UEM Kolkata
Founder’s Message:

Myself, Sayan Chakraborty, coming from a biological science background, I faced significant challenges during my 1st year of engineering.
At one point, I even thought of giving up—but I didn’t. I chose to activate my “never give up” attitude, and that decision changed my life.

If I had given up back then, I wouldn’t be here today as the Founder & CEO of Make Code Easy. My struggles became my biggest motivation to help others like me—students from non-computer science backgrounds who feel lost when they first encounter coding.

My main mission is simple: to transform non-CS students into job-ready professionals. From this spirit, Make Code Easy was born.
From Classroom to Career – We Make IT Easy!

From there, my dedication and resilience opened doors to multiple achievements...
